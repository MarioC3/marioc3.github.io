The mighty 5

Tools used: Photoshop, iBooks author & Sketch

I designed this iBook for one of my classes. Designing in iBooks was extremely hard due to it's limitations, but it also made push my limits. This iBook is really visual, and that's what I wanted to focus. I wanted the user to be amazed with the HQ picture I put in.

The interactions were minimal, since I wanted the images to speak by themselves. I started wire-framing the whole experience, then I optimize all the images in sketch and retouch hem in photoshop.
