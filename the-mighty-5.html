The mighty 5

UI/UX DEsign - Art Direction

OVERVIEW

The book is based on the idea that anyone can land in Las Vegas and head to St. George, Utah. The next day they can start their 4-5 day journey heading North and being able to visit “The Mighty Five” in one week and fly back out of Salt Lake City, Utah. The trek begins with:

- Zion National Park 
-Bryce Canyon National Park
-Capital Reef National Park
-Canyonlands National Park
-Arches National Park

Designing in iBooks was extremely hard due to it's limitations, but it also made push my limits. This iBook is really visual, and that's what I wanted to focus. I wanted the user to be amazed with the HQ pictures I put in. 

The interactions were minimal, since I wanted the images to speak by themselves. I started wire-framing the whole experience, then I optimize all the images in sketch and retouch them in photoshop.

Wireframes

We wanted this book to be really image-based, so, in our initial sketches, we decided to make the cover and the Table of Contents really visual.

The second sketch, was the interactions we were planning doing, since iBooks is somewhat limited, we decided to focus on some interactions, and to let go some interactions that we had on mind. We decided to include interactions that were going to bring value to the book and to the audience we were design it to.
